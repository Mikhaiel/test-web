About Abayashram

Abayashram is a transitional home for mentally ill women, dedicated to offering them a safe space to heal, grow, and reintegrate into society. Our vision is to spread peace and humanism across India.

Our Services

  • Counseling and therapy sessions
  • Rehabilitation programs
  • Job skill training
  • Medical support and wellness programs
